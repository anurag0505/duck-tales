Below Our Story

We all have stories inside us that we’re willing to tell to the right person. However, a barrier to sharing stories is often not having the right words.

Deck Tales is an adaptable creative resource that enables young people to tell their own stories and see them from a new perspective. The cards allow stories, real or imagined, to be told in an interactive way. Participants will be able to reflect on the story told from different characters’ perspectives and contribute their own thoughts and opinions about the story.

Deck Tales have been especially designed as a visual storytelling medium. By not including any words on the cards they are free of judgement and open to interpretation. This gives more opportunities for people to construct their stories.
